# Gamma AI Prompt - Corteza VC Pitch Deck

**Copy this entire prompt into Gamma.app:**

---

Create a venture capital pitch deck for Corteza, an AI-powered team memory platform that helps product teams capture and search decisions, context, and learnings.

## Slide Structure (15 slides):

**Slide 1 - Cover:**
- Title: "Corteza - Your Team's AI-Powered Memory"
- Subtitle: "Capturing decisions so teams never lose context"
- Add: "Confidential - [Current Month, Year]"

**Slide 2 - The Problem:**
- Title: "Teams Waste Hours Re-Explaining Past Decisions"
- 3-4 bullet points showing the pain:
  * Product teams make hundreds of decisions per quarter
  * Critical context gets lost in Slack threads, docs, and meetings
  * New team members spend weeks getting up to speed
  * Same questions get asked repeatedly ("Why did we choose X?")
- Add a quote: "We spent 30 minutes in a retro re-explaining decisions we'd already made"

**Slide 3 - The Cost:**
- Title: "The Hidden Cost of Lost Context"
- Show quantified impact:
  * Engineers spend 5-10 hours/week searching for information
  * Average 2-4 week onboarding time for context
  * Repeated decisions cost 20% of engineering time
  * Knowledge loss when team members leave
- Add: "This is a $15B+ problem in knowledge management"

**Slide 4 - Solution:**
- Title: "Corteza: Log Once, Search Forever"
- 3 key features with icons:
  * 🧠 AI-Powered Search: Ask in natural language, get instant answers
  * ⚡ Log from Anywhere: Slack, browser extension, dashboard
  * 🎯 Built for Product Teams: Capture decisions, context, and learnings
- Tag line: "Think of it as your team's external brain"

**Slide 5 - How It Works:**
- Title: "Simple Workflow, Powerful Results"
- Show 3-step visual flow:
  1. LOG: Team members capture decisions via Slack, extension, or dashboard
  2. AI PROCESSES: Claude + OpenAI embeddings create searchable memory
  3. SEARCH: Ask questions in natural language, get contextualized answers
- Add: "No training required. Teams start logging in < 5 minutes"

**Slide 6 - Product Demo:**
- Title: "See Corteza in Action"
- Show 2-3 screenshots:
  * Chat-first dashboard with AI search
  * Slack integration logging a decision
  * Browser extension popup
- Add callout: "30 AI searches free, then $29/month for unlimited"

**Slide 7 - Market Opportunity:**
- Title: "Massive TAM in Knowledge Management"
- Show market sizing:
  * TAM: $15B (Enterprise knowledge management software)
  * SAM: $3B (Team collaboration & decision-making tools)
  * SOM: $450M (Product teams at tech companies, 50-500 employees)
- Add: "50M+ product team members globally, growing 15% YoY"

**Slide 8 - Business Model:**
- Title: "Simple, Scalable SaaS Pricing"
- Show 3 pricing tiers:
  * FREE: 100 memories, 30 AI searches/month, 5 users → Land customers
  * TEAM: $29/month, unlimited AI search, 10 users → Primary monetization
  * BUSINESS: $99/month, 50 users, SSO, API → Enterprise expansion
- Add: "$9/month per additional user beyond plan limits"
- Bottom: "Currently in free beta with 50+ early adopters"

**Slide 9 - Traction:**
- Title: "Early Traction & Validation"
- Show key metrics in big numbers:
  * 50+ Beta workspaces signed up
  * 1,000+ team memories logged
  * 500+ AI searches performed
  * 4.8/5 user satisfaction score
  * Chrome extension just submitted
- Add: "100% organic growth, no paid marketing"

**Slide 10 - Competitive Landscape:**
- Title: "Positioned Between Two Categories"
- Show 2x2 matrix:
  * X-axis: "General Knowledge Management" → "Decision-Specific"
  * Y-axis: "Manual Search" → "AI-Powered"
  * Competitors in boxes:
    - Notion, Confluence (bottom-left): Manual, general
    - Guru, Slab (bottom-right): Manual, knowledge-focused
    - Mem, Reflect (top-left): AI-powered, personal
    - **CORTEZA (top-right, highlighted)**: AI-powered, team decision-focused
- Add: "Only tool purpose-built for team decision memory"

**Slide 11 - Why Now:**
- Title: "Perfect Timing for Corteza"
- Show 4 tailwinds:
  * Remote/hybrid work = scattered context
  * AI revolution makes semantic search accessible
  * Product teams growing faster than ever
  * Companies prioritizing knowledge retention
- Add quote: "The future of work is asynchronous and context-rich"

**Slide 12 - Go-to-Market:**
- Title: "PLG Motion with Enterprise Expansion"
- Show growth flywheel:
  * Phase 1 (Now): Product-led growth (PLG) - Free tier + Chrome extension
  * Phase 2 (6 months): Slack App Directory listing → viral growth
  * Phase 3 (12 months): Enterprise sales motion (SSO, on-premise)
  * Phase 4 (18 months): API platform for integrations
- Add: "30% month-over-month growth expected in Year 1"

**Slide 13 - Roadmap:**
- Title: "Product Roadmap - Next 18 Months"
- Show timeline:
  * Q1 2026: Chrome extension launch, pricing implementation
  * Q2 2026: Mobile apps (iOS/Android), Jira integration
  * Q3 2026: API platform, webhooks, custom integrations
  * Q4 2026: Enterprise features (SSO, on-premise, white-label)
- Add: "Focused on distribution channels and enterprise readiness"

**Slide 14 - Team:**
- Title: "Team & Advisors"
- Show founder info (customize with real info):
  * Founder name & title
  * Background (previous experience, relevant expertise)
  * Why you're the right person to build this
- Add: "Seeking technical co-founder and design lead"

**Slide 15 - The Ask:**
- Title: "Raising [Amount] to Scale Distribution"
- Use of funds breakdown:
  * 40% - Engineering (2-3 engineers, mobile, API)
  * 30% - Go-to-market (sales, marketing, Slack App Directory)
  * 20% - Product & Design (UX, enterprise features)
  * 10% - Operations & Infrastructure
- Key milestones with funding:
  * Reach 1,000 paying workspaces
  * Launch enterprise tier
  * Achieve $50K MRR
- Add contact: your email

**Slide 16 - Thank You:**
- Title: "Let's Build the Future of Team Memory"
- Subtitle: "Corteza - Where decisions become knowledge"
- Contact information
- Website: app.corteza.app

## Design Guidelines:

**Color Palette (Corteza Brand):**
- Primary: Black (#000000) and White (#FFFFFF)
- Accent: Dark gray (#1D1D1F) and light gray (#F8F9FA)
- Borders: #E1E4E8
- Success/highlights: Subtle green (#28a745)

**Typography:**
- Use Inter font family (modern, clean, readable)
- Bold headlines
- Clean, minimalist design

**Style:**
- Professional but modern
- Lots of white space
- Clean lines and borders
- Use simple icons (brain, lightning bolt, target, etc.)
- Minimal animations
- Data visualizations should be simple and clear

**Overall Vibe:**
- Technical but approachable
- Data-driven but human
- Bold vision but realistic execution
- Modern SaaS, not enterprise legacy

**Visual Elements:**
- Use simple illustrations over photos
- Charts should be clean and minimal (think Apple keynote style)
- Icons should be line-based, not filled
- Screenshots should show real product (not mockups)

---

Generate a complete pitch deck following this structure and design system. Make it compelling for venture capital investors who care about market size, traction, and scalability.
