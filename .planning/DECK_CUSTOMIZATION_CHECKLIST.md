## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

- [ ] **Don't inflate metrics** - VCs verify everything
- [ ] **Don't make unrealistic projections** - "10,000% growth" is red flag
- [ ] **Don't bad-mouth competitors** - be respectful but clear on differentiation
- [ ] **Don't overload slides** - max 3-5 bullets per slide
- [ ] **Don't use stock photos** - use real product screenshots
- [ ] **Don't forget contact info** - some VCs will reach out immediately

---

## 📧 Questions to Prepare For

VCs will likely ask these follow-up questions:

**Traction:**
- "What's your month-over-month growth rate?"
- "What's your retention/churn?"
- "Who are your most engaged customers?"

**Market:**
- "Why haven't big players (Notion, Confluence) solved this?"
- "What's your defensibility?"

**Team:**
- "Are you full-time on this?"
- "Who's your technical co-founder?" (if none: address this upfront)

**Business Model:**
- "What's your CAC and LTV?"
- "How are you acquiring customers?"
- "What's your expansion revenue strategy?"

**Product:**
- "What's your technical moat?"
- "How does the AI work exactly?"
- "Can you show me the product live?"

**Have answers ready!**
