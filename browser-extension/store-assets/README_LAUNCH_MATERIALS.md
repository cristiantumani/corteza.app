## 🆘 Common Questions

**Q: What if I get a negative review?**
A: See LAUNCH_KIT.md → Response Templates section. Respond professionally and offer help.

**Q: When should I post on social media?**
A: Best times: Tue-Thu, 8-10 AM in your timezone. Avoid Mon/Fri.

**Q: How often should I check Chrome Web Store?**
A: Day 1: Every 2 hours. Week 1: Daily. After: 2-3x per week.

**Q: Should I pay for ads?**
A: Not necessary on Day 1. Organic reach first. Consider ads after Week 1 if needed.

**Q: What if installation numbers are low?**
A: Don't panic! Focus on quality feedback. Improve based on input. Keep promoting.
