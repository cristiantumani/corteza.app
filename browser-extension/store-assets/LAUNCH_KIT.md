## 📝 Press Release / Blog Post

**Title:** Corteza Launches Browser Extension: Team Memory Available Everywhere

**Body:**

```markdown
# Corteza Launches Browser Extension: Team Memory Available Everywhere

**[City, Date]** — Corteza, the AI-powered team memory platform, today announced the launch of its Chrome browser extension, making it easier than ever for product teams to capture and search critical decisions.

## The Problem

Product teams make dozens of important decisions every week—about features, architecture, UX, and strategy. But those decisions often get buried in Slack threads, scattered across documents, or exist only in someone's memory. Teams waste hours re-explaining decisions they've already made.

## The Solution

Corteza's new browser extension allows teams to log decisions from any webpage without breaking their workflow. A simple keyboard shortcut (Ctrl+Shift+M) opens a quick-capture form that syncs instantly with the team's searchable memory.

"We built this because we felt the pain ourselves," says Cristian Tumani, founder of Corteza. "In one retrospective, we realized we'd spent 30 minutes in a meeting re-explaining a decision we'd made two months ago. That's time your team could spend building."

## Key Features

• **Universal Capture**: Log decisions from any webpage with a keyboard shortcut
• **AI-Powered Search**: Ask questions in natural language and get instant answers
• **Multiple Entry Points**: Browser extension, Slack bot, and web dashboard
• **No Slack Required**: Email-based authentication for teams without Slack
• **Semantic Search**: Powered by Claude AI and OpenAI embeddings

## Built for Modern Teams

Corteza is designed for product teams that move fast, work asynchronously, and onboard frequently. The platform helps teams:

• Stop re-explaining old decisions
• Onboard new members 10x faster
• Capture tribal knowledge before it's lost
• Search by meaning, not keywords

## Availability

The Corteza browser extension is available now for free in the Chrome Web Store. The platform also includes a Slack integration and web dashboard.

For more information, visit https://corteza.app

## About Corteza

Corteza is a team memory platform that helps product teams capture, organize, and search their decisions using AI. Founded in 2024, Corteza is built by product teams, for product teams.
```
